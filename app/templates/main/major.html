General FAQs

  • Are Liberty trust accounts FDIC insured?

    • Yes. Liberty trust is a member of the FDIC, so our deposit accounts are insured up to the maximum amount allowed by law. If you want to learn more about FDIC insurance limits

  • Are there fees for Optimum chartered private bank accounts?

    • There are no monthly maintenance or annual fees charged for Optimum chartered private bank accounts. Certain circumstances, like early withdrawal of CDs or insufficient funds, may incur penalties or fees. For more information about fees, please see account Terms and Conditions.

  • What is an "external account"?

    • Your external account is a bank account you have with another U.S. financial institution. It’s linked to your Optimum chartered private bank account so that you can transfer money between the two. Any external account that is linked to your Optimum chartered private bank account must be in an account holder’s name. This includes payment processing companies such as: Paypal, Dwolla, Coinbase, Venmo, Square, and Payday loan deposits.

  • Tell me about "test deposits."

    • For security reasons, we use test deposits as part of the external account verification process. 2 to 3 business days after you add a link to your Optimum chartered private bank account, we’ll make two test deposits of less than $1.00 into your external account. We do not use your money to make these test deposits.

    We’ll then ask you to log in to your Optimum chartered private bank account and report the amounts of those test deposits. After you have confirmed the test deposit amounts, you’ll be able to begin transferring money between your Optimum chartered private bank account and your external account. We use this method to verify each new external account. If you do not verify your external account within 99 days from when you opened your account, transfers will be restricted until you can verify your external bank account.

  • How do I reset my password?

    • You can reset your Optimum chartered private bank Online Banking password on our website. Simply select the Forgot UserID and/or Password link Log In from the Optimum chartered private bank Online Banking home page. You will be asked a few short questions to verify your identity. Then choose to have a one time pass code sent to you by email, phone, or text .The one time pass code will be active for 10 minutes.

  • How many Optimum chartered private bank Online Banking accounts can I have?

    • You are allowed to open and maintain any combination of 25 total accounts. Please note that there is a 3 account maximum for Dream Accounts.
